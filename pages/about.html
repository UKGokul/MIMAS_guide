What MIMAS is

MIMAS is a mesospheric ice microphysics and transport model designed to study noctilucent clouds (NLC/PMC) by coupling an Eulerian water vapour field with Lagrangian particle trajectories.

What it simulates

  • Water vapour evolution (advection + vertical diffusion)
  • Ice particle microphysics (Kelvin effect, growth/sublimation, sedimentation)
  • Solar photochemistry effects on H₂O (photolysis)
